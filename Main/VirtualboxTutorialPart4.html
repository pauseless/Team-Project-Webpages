Internal Networking

Isolating a network is cool and all. But what if you need two VM's to talk to eachother, but what if you need an VM to talk to another VM? Such as in the case of pentesting, getting a insecure VM to talk to a VM your going to pentest with.

That's where internal networking comes in! You can make a network where only the VM's can communicate to eachother, but never communicate onto or across your own network

Virtualbox Compatible Images

Virtualbox can be a little fussy when it comes to what files it accepts. Such as it refuses img files. But there is a hidden command that will let you turn any file into a VDI that Virtualbox can potentially load!
