What is a Snapshot?

Rewind the Clock

Snapshot's in VirtualBox allow you to save the state of a Virtual Machine. Essentially the way it was exactly functioning at that time

That means the machine can be reverted back to the time the snapshot was taken after it's suffered a critical failure, lost files, or you did something you didn't mean to do.

Be Warned However!

Snapshot's use a lot of storage! If you make too many you'll find yourself out of storage very quickly.
