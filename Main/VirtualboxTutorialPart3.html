Virtualization

Guest Additions, Import/Export and Network Isolation

Why do I need to know about this?

Guest Additions

Virtualbox has a lot of functions you can do, but many more tucked away under the hood. But you can bring them to the light by installing Guest Additions

Guest Additions adds a ton of functionality to the Virtual Machine, such as Shared Folders and proper resolution scaling.

Isolation

If there's one thing you don't want to happen while testing VM's, especially VM's with insecure operating systems. Its having your whole network comprimised alongside your real machine and any other devices

Being able to keep your VM off the network for that reason is more important than you think, and its a useful thing to learn as a result!
